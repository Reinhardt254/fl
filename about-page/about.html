ABOUT US

WHO WE

ARE

Plantech Kenya Ltd, based in Naivasha, Kenya, is a company specializing in sowing seedlings, and propagation. Our inception was driven after realizing that farmers were presenting challenges when germinating their plants. To address this, we took on the responsibility of this crucial farming process, offering customized solutions to meet the specific needs of each farmer. Presently, with over 5000 farmers relying on our products, we have witnessed significant growth and impact in the agricultural sector.

MISSION

Product:

Produce and distribute quality seedlings.

Economic Strategy:

Operate the company on a sustainable financial basis for profitable growth, enhance shareholders' value, and increase development and career opportunities for our employees.

Social Responsibility:

Operate the company in a manner that acknowledges Plantech's positive impact on the community, fostering innovative ways to enhance the quality of life through ethical business practices.

VISION

Assist farmers by maximizing their returns and providing them with quality products.

Innovation:
